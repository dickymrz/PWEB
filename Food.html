1. Rujak Cingur Surabaya

This food is the same as salad in general only in Surabaya using a mixture of rice cake, tofu, bean sprouts and cingur itself. Or in Indonesian Cingur is defined as a cow's nose.

2. Tahu Tek Surabaya

The Surabaya typical tofu is a quite legendary food in Surabaya. Food consisting of a mixture of rice cake, sprouts and tofu that is watered with seasoning paste brings a champion taste. Moreover, coupled with a sprinkling of fried onions and crackers will certainly add to its own enjoyment in enjoying the typical food on this one.

3. Pecel Semanggi Surabaya

This pecel is one of the healthiest foods you know, because it consists of a variety of healthy mixtures of vegetables such as papaya leaves, spinach leaves, kale and also of course clover leaves.

1. Hutan Bambu Surabaya

Just come to the forest which is located east of the city of Surabaya, which is on Jl. Raya Marina Asri, Keputih. Many visitors come to this tourist spot in Surabaya for a walk, relax, even for a pre-wedding photo shoot. In this one tourist spot, you can get Instagramable photos for free.

2. Ekowisata Mangroove Surabaya

It is located on Jl. Raya Wonorejo, Rungkut. This tourist spot in Surabaya is open to the public every weekend, starting at 8 a.m. to 4 p.m. Many visitors come to enjoy the green mangrove plants by walking on a wooden board path, or can also go along the mangrove forest area on the beach by boat.

3. Kenjeran Park Surabaya

His position is in the northeastern city of Surabaya. About 1 kilometer from Kenjeran Lama Beach. If you come to visit tourist attractions in Surabaya on this one, don't miss taking a picture near Suroboyo Bridge with the background of Kampung Bulak. The area is known as the 'Colorful Village'. Because the houses there are painted in bright bright colors! Great for photo locations!
